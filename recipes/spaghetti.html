Smoky Bacon Spaghetti

This recipe is so incredibly delish and will not hurt your wallet

May or may not contain bacon

May or may not be Italian

Ingredients

  • 5 slices of bacon
  • 1 savoy cabbage
  • 500g spaghetti
  • Tinned tomatoes

Steps

  1. Dice the bacon and fry it in a large lidded saucepan with a little oil until crispy on a medium heat.
  2. Slice the cabbage and add it to the bacon, fry for a few minutes, then add a little water and pop the lid on for a further few minutes to soften the cabbage.
  3. Finally, add the chopped tomatoes and turn the heat down to low.
  4. Boil the spaghetti according to packet instructions (saving a little pasta water).
  5. Once the spaghetti is cooked, add it to the sauce along with a little pasta water and mix thoroughly.
