Golden Vegetable Rice with Crispy Chickpeas

Golden Vegetable Rice with Crispy Chickpeas

The rice is golden and the vegetables are green

Ingredients

  • 500g of rice
  • 450g frozen veg
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1tbsp turmeric
  • 1 tin of chickpeas

Steps

  1. Turn the oven to 180.
  2. Cook everything in the oven
  3. Turn off the oven
