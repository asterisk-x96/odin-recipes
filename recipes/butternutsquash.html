Butternut Squash Curry

Autumnal favorite

Ingredients

  • 500g Butternut Squash
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Tin of coconut milk
  • 250g rice
  • 1 tbsp curry powder

Steps

  1. Add the butternut squash to a large saucepan with a little oil on a medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle over the curry powder and stir until all the butternut squash is coated.
  3. Pour in the tomatoes and then refill the tin full of water and add that too.
  4. Leave it to simmer for 5 minutes (or until the butternut squash is soft and then add the coconut milk - turn the heat down to low.
  5. Whilst the curry is simmering - cook the rice according to packet instructions.
  6. Season the curry if you wish.
